About Me

I'm a web developer with a thirst for knowledge and a knack for all things technical. After spending time with organizations in various industries, I have learned that web development suits me great and I have a thirst for more. This led me to learn to use Javascript on the front and back ends. I continue to learn, grow, and practice my craft as I expand into software and web development while learning the Ruby and Python programing languages.

I moved to Portland, OR in 2012 and I'm begining to fall in love with the Pacific Northwest and see why people generall don't leave. When I'm not programming, I enjoy spending time with my husband and two young boys, cooking, baking, paper-crafting, and learning to build the ultimate race track.
